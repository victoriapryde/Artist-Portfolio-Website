Victoria Pryde

New Zealand

Victoria graduated from the Dunedin School of Art 2017 with a BVA, majoring in photography & electronic arts.

She uses a variety of mediums in her practice, including: painting; digital art; and sculpture.

Driven by narrative, Victoria's work examines themes of memory, identity, and exploration. Her works are painterly, often depicting animals who step in to act as an earnest narrator.

Since graduating from art school, Victoria has been studying philosophy, with a variety of interest papers to further develop her practice.
Most recently, she has started learning web dev. She built this website from scratch!
